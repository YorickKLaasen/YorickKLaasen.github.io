About Drenched

Drenched is a Island Survival Game where you have to travel from island to island for resources.
the players objective is to survive for as long as possible and to freely explore as much as they want.

Why am i making Drenched?

Iam Developing Drench so that i can learn how to make certain mechanics/features and get better at writing code.

How Am i making Drench?

I will be using Unity 6 in 2D and Piskel for the sprites.
i will be using Scriptable Objects to easily make items and loottables.

The Idea

• The idea is to make a survival game where the world is made of islands and you have to travel over waters to get to other places
• Since islands have limited resources fishing is a big part of the game fishing gives different types of loot like just fish,rare items and others.
• I will be adding different types of hostile entities like wolfs,bears,sharks and some bosses.
• There are various biomes from jungles to swamps to arctic and deserts.

Gameplay

• Gameplay will be made simple so its easy to understand how to progress.
• The main loop will be collecting materials to upgrade your tools and equipment,
• using those tools to collect food and more materials so that the player can survive.

Systems

• I will be using a simple inventory where some items can stack and others can't the invetnory will have a few special slots with their own function like backpack and accessory.
• I will be using Noise Scale for generating the world each noise range is a biome so the maps are always different from the other.
• I will use a simple weight system to roll for loot in fishing or other mechanics by adding all dropchances/weights of the items and then rolling for loot
