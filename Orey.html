GameConcept

The concept of the game is to get the highest score possible just like in arcade games.
You get a higher score the longer you survive. there is obstacles that you have to avoid since they slow you down.
If you slow down to much a miner will catch you and you will lose.
There is also a Boss that appears at a score of 1000.
You can also choose to unlock achievements which dont give a reward but some people enjoy achievement hunting.
