Orey

Status: Finished

Finished On: 30th of May 2024

Description:

An arcade-style game I made with Adam El Zahiri, Ruben Stender, Deniz Akyurek, and Rinze Kuizenga.
This is an arcade-style game where the goal is to achieve the highest score possible.
You play as a rock running from a miner, avoiding obstacles to prevent being caught.
There is a boss fight at a score of 1,000. The game also includes achievements and a coin machine, similar to classic arcade games.

NeonDasher

Link to NeonDasher

Status: Finished

Finished On: 25th of October 2024

Description:

A mobile game I made with three others: Sam Schutten, Thom Hermelink, and Leon Bijker.
The goal of the game is to get as far as possible without crashing into obstacles or falling into the void.
You earn crystals for every 10 km of distance traveled, which can be used in the shop to purchase abilities or car skins.
